# Impact of Social Support on Mental Health

This visualization compares levels of social support (strong, moderate, weak) with mental health status (good, average, poor). # Average Age by Gender

This chart displays the average age distribution between males and females in the dataset. It provides a quick demographic overview and helps confirm that the dataset is relatively balanced across gender groups.

# Relationship Between BMI and Alcohol Consumption

This scatter plot explores the relationship between BMI and alcohol consumption. While there is no strong correlation, a mild upward trend suggests that some individuals with higher BMI also report higher alcohol intake, indicating potential lifestyle-related risk patterns.


# Quit Attempts and Substance Use Patterns

This chart plots average attempts to quit drinking against drinks per week. The increasing trend suggests that individuals with higher alcohol consumption attempt to quit more frequently, indicating cycles of dependence and relapse commonly associated with addiction.
